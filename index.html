Mission statement and links to project content.

We are Team O-VAC, a group of four Computer Engineering seniors at the University of California Santa Cruz. We are working on an AUV (Autonomous Underwater Vehicle) in collaboration with the Long Marine Lab to collect data and retreive soil samples from the ocean floor.
